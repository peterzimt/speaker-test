What makes our sessions different

Most "public speaking groups" are only about standing up and talking. Here, you learn and practise a specific technique each week, guided step by step — then put it to work on a real speech, with real feedback.

You'll speak every single time. No passive listening.

What we rotate through

Building speeches

Structure, angles, hooks and storytelling.

Connecting with your audience

Emotion, authenticity and presence.

Mastering delivery

Voice, rhythm, body language, eye contact and use of space.

Special situations

Improvisation, pitching yourself, speaking on camera, leading a group.

You'll also pick up practical frameworks: the Pyramid of Clarity (from idea to impact), the Five Canons of Rhetoric (Invention, Arrangement, Style, Memory, Delivery), and speech structures like Problem–Solution–Impact, Hook–Development–Revelation–Close, and Story–Message–Action.

How it works

  • Weekly sessions of 1.5 hours.
  • Small groups (8–15 people) for real practice and personal feedback.
  • A supportive, international community — all levels welcome.
  • Hands-on every time — a focus concept, practical drills, real speeches with constructive feedback, and tools to practise in daily life.

Why join

By the end of each block of sessions you'll be able to overcome nervousness and actually enjoy speaking, speak with clarity, structure and impact, adapt your style to work, interviews or social events, and engage any audience with confidence — all in a safe, supportive environment with people who want you to succeed.
